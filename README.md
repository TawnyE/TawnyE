## 🧩 Featured Projects

### 🔹 [TruthfulAC](https://github.com/TawnyE/TruthfulAC)
> A modern Minecraft **anti-cheat system** — free & open-source, available on Modrinth.  
> Designed to be lightweight, modular, and developer-friendly.

### 🔹 ControlBans
> A **moderation and banning system** offering fine-grained control, logging, and admin convenience.  
> Available on Modrinth. Built to integrate seamlessly with modern Minecraft server infrastructures.

### 🔹 [TawnyAntiVPN](https://builtbybit.com/resources/tawnyantiVPN)
> A **free VPN & proxy detection plugin**, available on BuiltByBit.  
> Multi-API consensus detection with ASN-level geolocation. Currently in its early stages — a more complete update is on the way.

### 🔹 [CodeControlAC](https://github.com/TawnyE/CodeControlAC) *(discontinued)*
> An earlier Minecraft **anti-cheat system** that laid the groundwork for TruthfulAC.  
> No longer maintained.
